Bachelor End Project

My BEProject is supervised by Prof.dr.Peter Hilbers at Eindhoven University of Technology. The overall goal of my project is to develop a on-line index and application(including visualization and simulation) platform for Recon2.2 model based on Django, an open-source framework that written by Python

ABOUT

Recon 2.2, which integrates various alternative versions with significant additional updates. In addition to re-establishing a consensus reconstruction, further key objectives included providing more comprehensive annotation of metabolites and genes, ensuring full mass and charge balance in all reactions, and developing a model that correctly predicts ATP production on a range of carbon sources.

Our Team

P.A.J.(Peter) Hilbers

Professor, Department of Biomedical Engineering at TU/e

Prof.Hilbers is the direct supervisor of this BEProject. His main research interests are large scale computer simulations, computational modeling and applications in biomedical engineering, systems biology, molecular systems, catalysis, and polymer chemistry.

Christopher T.Gong

Associate Professor, Department of Biomedical Engineering at NEU

Dr. Gong's present research is aiming to develop novel bone scaffold with 3D printing technology. His Lab is equiped with state-of-art instruments such as real-time PCR, high resolution microscope to investiage the biomaterial from the level of biology.
